About Us

Elmec Speciality Heaters Designs, Manufactures and Exports Cartridge Heaters, Hot Runner Coil Heaters, Nozzle Heaters, Manifold Heaters, Thermocouples & Hot Runner Systems.

Elmec Speciality Heaters is one of the most sought after brands in INDIA and it is now positioning itself well in the global markets. Elmec Speciality Heaters offers the most complete product line in the industry. With more than a million designs on file, we have the heaters, sensors and controls to meet your needs.
